PinkyBill

Workers' Compensation Medical Billing Platform

PinkyBill is a comprehensive Workers' Compensation medical billing platform built to simplify, streamline, and automate the end-to-end revenue cycle for providers and billing teams. Designed with compliance and efficiency in mind, PinkyBill manages claims, submissions, payments, and reporting in one secure environment.

Key Features

• Complete CMS-1500 claim creation and submission
• Integrated support for EDI, Fax, and Email submissions
• Automatic attachment of supporting documents (W-9, notes, reports)
• Real-time tracking for 999, 277, and 835 responses
• Multi-practice and multi-user management
• Secure cloud storage for all claim and patient files
• HIPAA-compliant architecture with full data encryption

Why Choose PinkyBill

PinkyBill was developed specifically for the Workers' Compensation industry, incorporating decades of billing experience. It ensures faster claim turnaround, accurate submissions, and transparent financial reporting — allowing providers to focus on patients while we handle the billing complexities.

Integrated Tools

PinkyMed: For private insurance and practice billing
AAFileShare: For document sharing and storage
Jopari / Carisk Integration: For clearinghouse submission automation
