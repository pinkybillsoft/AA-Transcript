PinkyMed

Private Insurance Medical Billing Platform

PinkyMed is a complete medical billing and practice management platform designed to handle private insurance claims efficiently and securely. Built on the same foundation as PinkyBill, it provides an all-in-one solution for healthcare professionals who deal with commercial insurance, PPO, and HMO claims.

Key Features

  • Automated CMS-1500 claim creation and electronic submission
  • Insurance eligibility verification and patient billing
  • ERA/835 posting and payment reconciliation
  • Denial management with real-time tracking and alerts
  • Customizable practice and provider dashboards
  • Built-in patient statements and payment reminders
  • Secure multi-tenant cloud hosting with role-based access

Benefits

PinkyMed helps clinics, practices, and billing companies reduce claim denials, improve turnaround time, and maintain transparency with detailed reporting tools. It streamlines both the billing and administrative workflows to maximize revenue collection and operational efficiency.

Who It’s For

PinkyMed is ideal for healthcare practices handling private insurance billing, including:

  • Medical offices and specialty clinics
  • Independent billing services
  • Multi-location practices
  • Medical groups managing PPO and commercial plans
