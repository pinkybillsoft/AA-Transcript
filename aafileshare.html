AAFileShare

Cloud-Based Document Management for Healthcare and Legal Professionals

AAFileShare is a secure, HIPAA-compliant file management and sharing platform built to simplify document handling between doctors, attorneys, staff, and billing teams. It centralizes document storage and sharing in one place—so your sensitive files are always accessible, organized, and protected.

Key Features

• Secure cloud storage with role-based access permissions
• Instant file sharing between doctors, attorneys, and staff
• Supports multiple file types (PDF, Word, Images, Scans, ZIPs)
• Organize documents by patient, claim, or case folders
• Upload via drag-and-drop with progress tracking
• Automatic PDF conversion for uploaded documents
• Audit trail and activity logs for compliance
• Accessible from desktop, tablet, or mobile

Why Choose AAFileShare

AAFileShare eliminates the need for insecure email attachments or third-party sharing links. Every document is encrypted at rest and in transit, ensuring HIPAA and privacy compliance. Designed specifically for healthcare and legal industries, it keeps your case documentation connected across billing, claims, and medical reporting.

Integration

Fully integrated with PinkyBill and PinkyMed to attach claim-related documents (CMS1500, reports, W-9, notes, and EOBs) directly from AAFileShare. This creates a seamless ecosystem for medical billing and documentation management.
