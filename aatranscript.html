Medical Billing, Transcription, and Administrative Services

AA Transcript offers medical billing, administrative services and transcription services to medical professionals in Nevada and California. Specializing in the workers' compensation field, we are experienced in streamlining medical documentation and boosting physicians' productivity. We'll take care of the records, documents, faxes, letters and charts so that you can focus on patient care.

Reports

We make initial reports, follow-up reports, supplemental and MMI/permanent and stationary reports. We have professionals who are trained in ACOEM/MTUS guidelines and provide the impairment rating as per the guidelines.

RFAs

Our team of professionals makes RFAs according to the guidelines reducing the number of denials. We do not only make the RFAs but fax the RFAs to insurance companies on the doctor's behalf.

Review of Medical Records

We review the medical records, whether it is one page or thousands of pages (hospital records, AME/PQME reports, etc.), and provide comments on the records by reviewing the case thoroughly.

Denial Rebuttals

We have a specialized team who constantly studies the IMR overturns. We have thousands of IMR decisions in our database. Our team will rebut your denials and increase the number of certifications.

Administrative Services

We provide administrative services, the essential, supporting tasks that keep an organization running smoothly, encompassing a wide range of functions like scheduling, managing communications, data entry, office management, record keeping, and even handle your faxes and emails. This helps you reduce costs, improve efficiency and gain access to specialized expertise. This allows your internal staff to focus on core business functions while also providing scalability and better risk management through access to a larger talent pool and up-to-date technology.
