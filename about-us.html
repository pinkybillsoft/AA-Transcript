About Us

AA Transcript is a medical billing and transcription company based in Las Vegas, Nevada, providing services to doctors across Nevada and California. We specialize in the workers' compensation field with a team of trained professionals in ACOEM and MTUS guidelines. Our association brings cost reductions, performance improvements, and new ways of working—tailored to each client.

Experienced Billing Professionals

Our billing team consists of professionals with more than 20 years of experience in medical billing, serving multiple specialties including dermatology, orthopedics, internal medicine, and chiropractic.

Call Center & Administrative Support

We provide high-quality telephone and web-based services. Our call center professionals work around the clock managing collections and AR recovery.
